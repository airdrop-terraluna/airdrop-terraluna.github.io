Who are the founders of the Terra (LUNA) project?

Project Terra (LUNA) was founded in 2018 by Do Kwon and Daniel Shin of Terraform Labs. Do Kwon, who is also the CEO of Terraform Labs, is known for his investments and initiatives in the blockchain space. Daniel Shin is the founder and CEO of Chai, a major payment firm in Asia, as well as the Terra (LUNA) project.
